## Which AI-service niches are opening right now?

Three windows opened in this week's snapshot:

**1. End-to-end AI automation — the "novelty" lane is wide open (75/100).**
Saturation in this niche is only ~13%: almost nobody is advertising AI automation with a novelty/discovery angle, while at least one new entrant launched in the last 30 days. Translation: businesses want workflows automated, and the market of people *selling* that outcome is still thin.

**2. AI video generation — "transformation" is heating (67/100).**
The ads that run here don't sell software; they sell *becoming someone who ships content*. One-third saturation, fresh launches inside 30 days. If your marketing still uses stock photos, this is the cheapest upgrade window we can see in the data.

**3. AI-augmented product building — "transformation" again (60/100).**
The story that works is "transform your capability in weeks," not feature lists. Half the lane's advertisers launched recently — motion without crowding.

Meanwhile, lanes like **WhatsApp AI agents** and **fractional CTO** score *stable*: demand exists, few advertisers compete, and the winning angle there is **pain-point relief** — "tired of X? here's the fix" — rather than novelty.
